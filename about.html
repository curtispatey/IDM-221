Who We Are

We are a student led design group, founded by myself, Curtis Patey, with goals of working with Drexel students to help visualize their product goals. I came to Philadelphia, PA from Nashville, TN to attend Drexel University. I quickly fell in love with the city and my peers on campus. After an extensive journey through engineering, I realized that I needed to find something that matched my interpersonal skills. That led me to the Interactive Digital Media program where I am focusing on UX research.

Our Mission

Cog creative group is a studio focused on working closely with clients to develop meaningful design solutions. Located in Philadelphia, PA, we have a passion for working with local clients at Drexel University. We specialize in UX research to guide our design process in a meaningful and impactful way for our clients and their products.
